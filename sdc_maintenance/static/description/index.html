Key Features

Maintenance request

Maintenance managers can select equipment with a problem, describe the problem, and assign a specific technician to do the maintenance request. When the maintenance request is validate, the responsible generate work order.

Tracking PM

Maintenance managers can plan preventive maintenance, describe what can be happen, and assign a specific technician to check the preventive maintenance. Also, the responsible can receive notification about next PM.

Tracking CM

Maintenance managers can plan preventive maintenance, describe what can be happen, and assign a specific technician to check the preventive maintenance. Also, the responsible can receive notification about next PM.

Equipments

Equipment most commonly refers to a set of tools machine commonly used to achieve a particular objective. Each equipment can be associated to mantenance, work order, CM, PM...

Spare part

A spare part, is an interchangeable part that is managed and kept in an inventory and used for the repair or replacement of failed units for various equipment through maintenance program.

Checklist

Identify maintenance actions on various equipment and incorporate that into the preventive maintenance program activities that result in the greatest benefit.
